Welcome to the Premises Register

This service contains details of premises licensed by a local authority in Great Britain for the purposes of providing gambling services. These include premises such as betting shops, bingo clubs, and casinos.

This register is updated on a monthly basis. The information on the register is provided to the Commission by the relevant local authority. If you would like the most up to date information regarding premises in a particular area, you should contact the relevant local authority directly.

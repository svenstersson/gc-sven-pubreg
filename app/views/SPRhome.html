Welcome to the Sanctions Register

This service contains details of regulatory action, including sanctions, taken against a licence holder by the Gambling Commission.

Regulatory action may be taken against holders of operating or personal licences. Regulatory actions remain on this register for three years from the date of the action. After this time has expired, the action is removed from the register.

Our registers

The Commission maintains a range of registers which the public may use to find information about the operators and individuals that we licence, any regulatory that we have taken against a licence holder, and premises which are used to deliver gambling services.

Find information about an operator

This register contains operators we currently licence, those who previously held a licence, and applicants for an operating licence.

Go to Operator Public Register

Find information about an individual

This register contains individuals who currently hold a personal licence and those who previously held a personal licence.

Go to Personal Public Register

Find information about a regulatory action

This register contains details of any sanctions or other regulatory actions taken against a licence holder in the last three years.

Go to Sanctions Register

Find information about a gambling premises

This register contains details of any premises licensed by a local authority in Great Britain for the provision of gambling services. The information in this register is provided to the Commission by the relevant local authority.

Go to Premises Register
